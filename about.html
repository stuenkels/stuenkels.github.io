About


Updated 4/16/24
       Always having a passion for technology led to many years of reading every book and article I could find, watching every video, and asking every class I could have given me a bit of knowledge about technology. This blog, while aiding me in my attempts to document all my projects and experiments, also serves as a reference guide, filled with things I wish I knew when I was first starting out. Topics will cover everything from multi-threaded socket servers in C, network configuration and setup, and even into the world of electronics with PCB design and engineering. Any code I write about will be available on my Github, as well as any other resources. This site is a simple static HTML page thrown together in one night, I am not a front end developer, I much rather write assembly than HTML. The downside of such a simple page is there will be no search, or comments, and I have no plans to implement them.
